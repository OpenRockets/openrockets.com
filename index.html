OpenRockets Foundation: 2025 Summer Impact Report

OpenRockets Foundation | September 5, 2025

The summer of 2025 marked a transformative period for the OpenRockets Foundation. Our community grew, our impact expanded, and we achieved significant milestones that position us for continued success in empowering the next generation of developers.

Key Milestones Achieved

  • 30+ Members Strong: Reached the first 30 members in the OpenRockets GitHub organization, representing multiple countries including Sri Lanka, India, and the Maldives.
  • 501(c)(3) Status: Became a fiscally sponsored 501(c)(3) public charity registered in the United States of America, enabling greater transparency and trust.
  • Community Engagement: Hosted two in-person table meetups with both working professionals and students involved in OpenRockets, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing.
  • Open-Source Licenses: Established two open-source licenses to help teenagers increase the visibility of their projects online and gain recognized credentials through opensocial.online platform.
  • CityofGits Launch: Launched CityofGits, an open-source 3D world project developed collaboratively by all members of the OpenRockets Foundation.
  • NASA Space Apps Support: Supported five NASA Space Apps Challenge Sri Lanka projects through funding, pitching guidance, and volunteer assistance.

HackClub DayDream Sri Lanka

Coming September 27th
LIVE EVENT

Create amazing games in just 24 hours! Join developers across Colombo and various locations throughout Sri Lanka for an epic game development hackathon inspired by HackClub's global maker community.

What is HackClub DayDream?

DayDream is a 24-hour game development jam where teenage developers and makers come together to create incredible games from scratch. Inspired by HackClub's philosophy of learning by building, this event encourages participants to explore game development, experiment with new tools, and collaborate with fellow builders.

What You'll Do:

  • Form teams with other developers (or work solo)
  • Brainstorm and design your game concept
  • Build your game using Unity, Unreal Engine, Scratch, JavaScript, or any tool you prefer
  • Create graphics, sounds, and gameplay mechanics
  • Present your creation to judges and fellow participants
  • Win awesome prizes and gain recognition in the community

Perfect For:

  • Beginners: New to coding? We'll provide tutorials and mentors!
  • Intermediate: Ready to try new frameworks and tools
  • Advanced: Push the boundaries with complex game mechanics
